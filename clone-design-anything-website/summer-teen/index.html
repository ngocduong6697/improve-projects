LIFE TALK

Confronting issues with your Folks

When it comes to being honest with your parents, it can be tough. You're afraid they might just scold you for something when you're finally being honest with them. So here's a trick to get it out of your chest, with no fear.

Approach them calmly about a situation you'd want to tell them. Try to collect your thoughts prior to talking to them so you're not stumbling all over the place. If you have to, write down your points so you can discuss them properly
